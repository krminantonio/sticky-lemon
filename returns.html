returns


It's possible to return your Sticky Lemon product within 30 days.


Please note, the returned product should be sent back in the exact same conditions as how you received it. All returned products will be checked. They should be unused and packed in the original packaging.


If a product is damaged when it arrives, we advise you to contact us directly. Please send us photos of the damage which we will check for you. We will contact you about a solution as soon as possible.


If you return a product, the costs are for your own account. We advise you to send the product by registered post. We keep you responsible for the return.


For international orders (outside the European Union), you'll be responsible for the costs of the return as well as for the additional costs such as VAT and import duties.

-

Please enclose our return form within the package and send the package to the following address.


Sticky Lemon Rouaanstraat 8 9723 CD Groningen The Netherlands
